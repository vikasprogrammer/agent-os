The one invariant

Every real action stays in bounds — by design, not by trust.

A send, a charge, a deploy, a database write — each one passes through a single mediated gate before it can touch the world. Remove the gate and there is no way around it. That's what makes autonomy safe to put in production.

  • Your policy decides what's green, what needs a human, and what's never allowed — edited live, applied to running sessions instantly.
  • Risky calls pause for a person with a rich approval card; approve once with "Always" and it learns a rule, safely.
  • Agents act as accountable humans — a chat-triggered run is signed by the teammate who asked, not a faceless bot.
  • Everything is on the record — an append-only audit log you can filter by session, type, or person.
